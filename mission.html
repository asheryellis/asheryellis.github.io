MISSION

Our mission is to empower those who wish to serve.


How members benefit:

Connections

JSCN is all about community. Whether it's another member from your hometown or someone you share a niche interest with, JSCN enables those connections.

Opportunities

In JSCN, we empower one another by collaborating and sharing opportunities — whether you're looking for hackathon teammates, research projects, or internships.

Discourse

Understanding our country's involvements is essential to leading in them. In JSCN, we share ideas and discuss the key news stories that shape our the armed services.
